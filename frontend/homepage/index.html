    About Us



    The impact of mentorship on an individual's success is well documented. Research has also shown that self-efficacy is one of the biggest determining factors of goal completion.


    We exist because we believe that, especially for young black and brown boys, access to mentorship and networking opportunities with men who look like them or have shared experiences is necessary but severely lacking. In addition, black boys have consistently been socialized to limit their view of potential career paths in which they can find success. Therefore, our objectives include:


    • Facilitating a safe, structured virtual environment for male minority students and professionals of all levels to engage one other

    • Increasing self-efficacy within young black males through increased increased interaction with and awareness of successful black men.

    • Providing exposure to a broad range of career paths.

    • Serving as centralized portal for employment/internship opportunities, scholarships and grants specifically targeted to benefit black men.
